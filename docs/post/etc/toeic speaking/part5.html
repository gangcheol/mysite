0. Intro

- : 준비시간 45초, 답변시간 60초

- part5 이론강의영상

(1) 준비시간 전략

(1)-1. 문제 해석과 이해

  • keypoint : “문제에 대한 적절합 답변을 하는 것!”

  • 15초 이내로 문제 해석을 끝낸내는 것이 가장 좋음

(1)-2. 아이디어 생각하기

  • “파트 5 만능 문장, 파트3 만능 문장50가지” 를 이용하여 2문장 이상을 준비시간에 생각해내자

  • 파트 5 만능문장

(1)-3. 노트 테이킹

  • 5글자 이상 되는 단어는 앞의 4-5글자만 쓰고, 동사 위주로만 필기하자.

(2) 답변시간 전략

- 전반적인 순서 : 서론(결론) \(\to\) 이유1 \(\to\) 예시1\(\to\) 이유2 \(\to\) 예시2 \(\to\) Therefore, I agree with the statement~

- 할 수 있다면 만능문장과, 모범답안을 계속 모방해서 연습하자(고득점 전략!)

\(\divideontimes\) 동문서답금지 \(\to\) 외운 답변을 문제에 적절하지 않게그대로 말하면 큰 실점이 된다.

(3) 목표 레벨별 전략

- 130 ~ 150 (IH) : 문제에 적합한 답변을 채점자가 알아 듣게만 하자 \(\to\) 모범 답안의 70 ~ 80%의 퍼포먼스

- 160이상 (AL 이상) : 파트5 만능 문장을 2문장 이상 다양하게 이용해서 말한다.

- 파트 3의 만능 문장 50의 쉬운 문장들도 함께 이용한다.

- 만능 문장을 적용해서 답변하는 연습 (30 set구비)
  • 130 + vs 160 : 구체성의 차이!
    • A. IH 답변 : From my experience, I studied overseas
    • B. AL 답변 : From my experience, I studied in Japan as an exchange student last year.

(4) part5 만능 문장

A. 경험 & 교육

  1. They can learn new things.

  2. They can meet new people and expand their network.

  3. They can have a lot of (new) experience and broaden their perspective.

  4. They can’t make good decisions because they are not mature enough.

B. 집중

  1. They will be distracted / if a teacher is not strict : strict(엄격한)

  2. They can’t focus on their studies/work.

  3. They can’t get good grades at school.

  4. They will fall behind in class.

  5. They can’t work efficiently.

C. 돈

  1. They can save money.

  2. The cost of living is too high.

  3. They can’t make a living.

  4. I can get a high(er) salary.

  5. The cost of N is too expensive.

  6. It’s waste of money.

  7. That’s good investment because it makes lives better.

D. 혼자 vs 같이

  1. They can focus better.

  2. They will not be distracted by others.

  3. They can set their own schedule.

  4. They can have more freedom.

  5. They feel more comfortable.

  6. It’s fun and entertaining.

  7. They can get information and share it with other people.

  8. It feels more like a family.

E. 기술

  1. They can get a lot of (useful information/latest information) on the Internet.

    • 기기나 인터넷 상에서 하는 것 앞에는 전치사 ‘on’ 을 쓴다.

  2. They can communicate with their friends anytime anywhere on their smartphones.

  3. It’s faster and convenient.

  4. There is a lot of inaccurate information on the internet so it’s not reliable.

  5. it is very distracting for S so S can’t focus on their studies/work.

  6. I can get responses right away

  7. I can understand the feeling of the speaker more accurately.

F. 특징

  1. They can make a friendly (work) atmosphere.

  2. They can communicate with others better.

  3. They can be good team players and make good relationships with others.

  4. They can have a good reputation.

  5. They can be very influential.

  6. They can motivate others.

  7. Everything is always changing and there is a lot of competition.

  8. They face a lot of challenges and difficulties.

  9. He is able to handle a variety of situation due to his N.

  10. They have a lot of experience/knowledge.

G. 업무 환경 & 기업 성공

  1. Employees can work more efficiently and productively.

  2. Employees can be more satisfied with their jobs.

  3. It can make a better work environment.

  4. They might appear less professional.

  5. Customers will fell satisfied and remain loyal.

  6. It will attract more customers.

  7. The business will be more successful.

  8. People frequently use N so it will be very effective.

H. 스트레스 & 건강

  1. It relieves their stress and they can relax.

  2. It is good for their (Physical / mental) health.

  3. It is not good for their health.

I. 환경

  1. It is good for environment.

  2. Pollution is a serious issue these days.

  3. It can make a cleaner environment.

  4. We will be able to protect the environment.
